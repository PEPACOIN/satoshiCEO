About Project

Satoshi CEO is a project that will run on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network in homage to the mysterious creator of Bitcoin, Satoshi Nakamoto.

Why Satoshi CEO? Satoshi Nakamoto is the creator of Bitcoin and blockchain technology. Although his identity remains a mystery, his creation has revolutionized the financial and technological world. Satoshi CEO is created in tribute to his genius and legacy.
